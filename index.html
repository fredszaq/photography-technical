Troubleshooting

Blury picture

  • reduce exposition time
  • reduce hand shaking
  • use a VR system
  • use a tripod/monopod
  • use a sharper lens (do the the rest first)

Underexposed picture

  • augment exposition time
  • augment apperture
  • augment sensitivity
  • turn on the lights
  • use a flash
  • avoid contrejour

Burned picture

  • reduce sensitivity
  • reduce aperture
  • reduce exposure time
  • use an ND filter
